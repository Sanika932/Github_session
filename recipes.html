Vegetable Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt & pepper

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add vegetables and stir-fry.
  2. Add rice and mix well.
  3. Pour in soy sauce, season with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve hot.
